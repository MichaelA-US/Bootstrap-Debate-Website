Types of Debate

In debate we have a variety of different groups in which you can pick is the most interesting and enjoyable to you.

Public Forum

Public Forum is a slow-paced, formal debate that is when four individuals compete in a debate with two people both in their own team, and each group gives short speeches with sections for questions.

Policy

Policy debate is a fast-paced debate when two teams of two people compete in a debate with short speeches and blocks of questions.

Congressional

Congressional debate is when an individual participates in a room with a group of people 10+ and gives speeches on few bills on why they should pass or fail it.

Extempt

Extemporaneous debate is when an individual is given a short period of time to speak on a topic and prepare for it in a short speech.
